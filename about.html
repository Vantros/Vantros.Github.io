About Us

Creative Builders. Technical Innovators.

Little Game Productions is a tight-knit team of builders, circuiteers, and designers working together inside Rec Room to craft innovative, fun, and immersive experiences. With a focus on high-quality environments, advanced CV2 systems, and a collaborative team culture, we push the boundaries of what’s possible. Whether it’s a competitive PvP map or a scenic hangout world, our goal is to bring ideas to life with style and precision.
