About Little Game Productions

Little Game Productions is a creative Rec Room team made up of builders, scripters, and designers who are passionate about crafting unique virtual worlds. We specialize in building fun, functional, and polished experiences using the full power of Rec Room Studio, CV2 circuits, and collaborative imagination.

Our goal is simple: turn even the smallest idea into something unforgettable. Whether it’s a competitive PvP map, a cozy hangout room, or a fully interactive experience, we approach each project with care, creativity, and teamwork.

  • ✔ Mastery of CV2 circuits and logic
  • ✔ High-quality building and environment design
  • ✔ Rec Room Studio workflows and optimization
  • ✔ Collaborative spirit and community engagement
